What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where criminals pretend to be trusted organizations or people to trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, OTPs, or bank details.

Common Types of Phishing

  • Email phishing (fake emails)
  • SMS phishing (Smishing)
  • Phone call phishing (Vishing)
  • Fake websites & login pages
  • Social media impersonation

If You Are a Victim

  • Change passwords immediately
  • Contact your bank or service provider
  • Block cards / accounts if needed
  • Scan your device for malware
